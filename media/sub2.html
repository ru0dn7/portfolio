  • Manjang Cave

    It is a lava cave of a large scale even on a global scale. There are many lava caves distributed around the world, but lava caves like Manjanggul Cave, which were formed hundreds of thousands of years ago and whose internal shape and topography are well preserved, are rare, so they have great academic and conservation value.
