about jeju

The name Samdado was given because there were many winds, rocks, and women.
It contains the history of the lives of Jeju people who wisely and tenaciously overcame the natural environment.

wind

Is there any other place with such diverse and unique names for winds as Jeju? Hanibaram, Nophanibaram, Nopsaebaram, Saetbaram, Maparam, Dongmaparam, Galbaram, Seotbaram, Seothanibaram, Yangdusaebaram, Yangbaram. Jeju people have created a unique wind culture by fighting, confronting, avoiding, sometimes appeasing, and utilizing the winds that come with all sorts of names.

rock

Jeju is a volcanic island covered in rocks, not only the land and mountains that make up the island, but also the coastline surrounding the island. The total length of Jeju's stone walls is estimated to be 36,000 kilometers, which is almost as long as it can go around the Earth. How amazing is it that such a long stone wall exists on an island that is just a tiny dot on the globe?

female

Why are there so many goddesses on Jeju Island? Those who go out to sea to fish, those who go out to the fields to weed, those who go out to the market to sell, those who look for firewood and carry water are all women, so how can it not be called the 'Island of Women'? The name of the Island of Women reflects the long history of Jeju Island, which has been led by women.
